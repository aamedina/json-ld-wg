Subject: [DRAFT HPN] The JSON-LS 1.0 Recommendations Superseded by JSON-LD 1.1

The JSON-LD Working Group has announced that the JSON-LD 1.0 and the JSON-LD 1.0 Processing Algorithms and API specifications, both published in 2014, have been superseded by JSON-LD 1.1, respectively JSON-LD 1.1 Processing Algorithms and API, published in July this year. These newer documents provide 1.0 compatibility and they are recommended for new adoption in place of the 1.0 specification.
